Sage Mosurinjohn is a creative jack-of-all-trades. Following her love of writing, she earned a bachelor’s degree in journalism from Carleton University. For several years after she worked in communications before discovering her real passion: design. She is currently enrolled in her second year of Algonquin College’s graphic design program. She now has experience in layout design, typography, illustration, photography and anything she can get her hands on.
